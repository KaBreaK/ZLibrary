# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the **ZLibrary** application processes user data.

**1. No Sensitive Data Storage**
The application does not download, store, or process logins, passwords, or any other identifying information.

**2. Storage of Authorization Codes**
The application stores only authorization codes that are necessary to download games from various platforms.
- These codes are used solely for authentication purposes.
- They do not contain any personal or sensitive information.
- The data is stored locally on the user's device and is not transmitted to any external servers.
